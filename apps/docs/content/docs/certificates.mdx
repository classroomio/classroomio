---
title: How to Create Certificates
description: Configure certificate rules for a course, set completion requirements, and let students download their certificates.
---

Certificates recognize students for completing a course. You configure the rules on a per-course basis — how much of the course must be completed, whether a final exercise must be passed, and when certificates expire. Students can then download their certificate from the **Certificates** tab in the LMS.

:::warning[Paid plan required]
Certificate features require a paid plan. The controls are visible on free plans but disabled.
:::

## Configure certificate rules

Open your course in the admin dashboard and go to the **Certificates** tab.

![Certificate rules panel with the download toggle and completion threshold](/docs/certificates-rules.webp)

<Steps>
<Step>
### Enable downloadable certificates

Turn on **Allow students download certificate**. When this is off, no certificate is generated even if students meet the requirements.
</Step>

<Step>
### Set a completion threshold

Enter a percentage in **Minimum completion (%)**. A student must complete this share of the course to qualify. Defaults to 100%.
</Step>

<Step>
### Require a final exercise (optional)

Select an exercise from **Final exercise (optional)**. When set, the student must pass that specific exercise (best attempt counts) in addition to meeting the overall completion percentage. Set **Minimum score on final exercise (%)** to the minimum passing score for that exercise.
</Step>

<Step>
### Set a deadline (optional)

Enter a date and time in **Certification deadline**. Students who complete the course after this date do not receive a certificate.
</Step>

<Step>
### Add a custom email message (optional)

Enter text in the email message field. This message is included in the notification email sent to students when they earn their certificate.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Customize the design and add signatures

Set up how the certificate looks before the course goes live, so the first student to qualify gets the finished
version. On the **Certificates** tab, click **Customize design** to open the certificate editor.

![Certificate design editor showing the template picker and live preview](/docs/certificates-design-editor.webp)

<Steps>
<Step>
### Pick a template and accent color

Choose a **Template** and an accent color to match your brand. A live preview shows the result as you change it.
</Step>

<Step>
### Add signatories and signatures

Under **Signatories**, turn on **Show signatory 1** and fill in **Signatory 1 name** and **Signatory 1 role**, then
use **Signature image** to upload a signature graphic. You can enable a second signatory the same way.

![Signatories section with name, role, and signature image upload](/docs/certificates-signatories.webp)
</Step>

<Step>
### Set the certificate ID format (optional)

Set a **Certificate ID format** so every issued certificate carries a unique, verifiable reference.
</Step>
</Steps>

:::info
Finalize the design and signatures before you publish the course. Certificates are generated from the current design
when a student qualifies, so getting it right up front means no one receives an unfinished certificate.
:::

## What students see

Once a student meets all the configured requirements, their certificate appears in the **Certificates** tab when they open the course in the LMS. They can view and download it from there.

![Student certificate view with download buttons](/docs/certificates-student-download.webp)
