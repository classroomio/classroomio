Certificates recognize students for completing a course. You configure the rules on a per-course basis — what score is required, whether a final exercise must be passed, and when certificates expire. Students can then download their certificate from the **Certificates** tab in the LMS.

## Configure certificate rules

Open your course in the admin dashboard and go to the **Certificates** tab.

<Steps>
<Step>
### Enable downloadable certificates

Turn on **Allow students to download their certificate**. When this is off, no certificate is generated even if students meet the requirements.
</Step>

<Step>
### Set a completion threshold

Enter a percentage in **Minimum score to receive certificate**. A student must reach this overall course score to qualify. Defaults to 100%.
</Step>

<Step>
### Require a final exercise (optional)

Select an exercise from **Final exercise**. When set, the student must pass that specific exercise in addition to meeting the overall threshold. Set **Minimum exercise score** to the minimum passing score for that exercise.
</Step>

<Step>
### Set a deadline (optional)

Enter a date and time in **Certificate deadline**. Students who complete the course after this date do not receive a certificate.
</Step>

<Step>
### Add a custom email message (optional)

Enter text in the email message field. This message is included in the notification email sent to students when they earn their certificate.
</Step>
</Steps>

## What students see

Once a student meets all the configured requirements, their certificate appears in the **Certificates** tab when they open the course in the LMS. They can view and download it from there.

<Callout type="info" title="Issuing certificates manually">
  Admins can also issue a certificate to a specific student manually using the **Issue Certificate** button at the top of the Certificates tab, regardless of whether the student met the automatic requirements.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warn" title="Paid plan required">
  Certificate features require a paid plan. The controls are visible on free plans but disabled.
</Callout>
