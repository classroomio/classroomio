A **course landing page** is the public page for one course. Prospects read the description, see pricing, and click **Enroll** if enrollment is open. This is separate from your [academy landing page](/docs/how-to-guides/org-landing-page), which is the academy homepage.

## Open the editor

<Steps>
<Step>
### Select a course

From the admin dashboard, open **Courses** and click the course you want.
</Step>

<Step>
### Open Landing page

In the course sidebar, click **Landing page** to open the editor.
</Step>

<Step>
### Edit sections

Update sections such as hero, description, requirements, pricing, instructors, and footer. Click **Save** when finished.
</Step>

<Step>
### Preview

Use the preview control next to **Save** to see the page. Confirm behavior on the live academy for links and enroll buttons.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Editor sections

| Section | What you configure |
| --- | --- |
| **Hero** | Title, subtitle, primary CTA (e.g. Enroll, Get Started) |
| **Description** | Course overview for prospects |
| **Requirements** | Prerequisites or audience notes |
| **Pricing** | Price and currency |
| **Instructors** | Instructor cards |
| **Footer** | Supporting links and copy |

Hero and pricing interact with [course enrollment](/docs/how-to-guides/course-enrollment): a free course with **Allow New Students** on shows an open **Enroll** button; paid or closed courses route visitors through payment or invites.

## Pricing and currency

Set the course price in the **Pricing** section. ClassroomIO supports these currencies today:

| Currency | Code |
| --- | --- |
| US Dollar | USD |
| Nigerian Naira | NGN |

<Callout type="info" title="Other currencies">
  If you need a currency outside USD and NGN, that requires product support today. Pick the closest supported currency or keep the course free and handle payment outside ClassroomIO.
</Callout>

## Publish and Allow New Students

Landing page content can be ready before you open enrollment:

| Course **Settings** | Effect on landing page |
| --- | --- |
| **Published** | Page is live on your academy |
| **Allow New Students** | Public **Enroll** works (for free courses) |
| **Price > 0** | Enrollment requires payment or a valid invite |

See [How Course Enrollment Works](/docs/how-to-guides/course-enrollment) for closed cohorts and invite-only access.

## Preview vs live site

The in-app preview shows layout and copy. Test **Enroll**, external links, and payment on your live academy URL (subdomain or [custom domain](/docs/how-to-guides/custom-domain)) before sharing publicly.

<Callout type="warn" title="Org landing page preview">
  The org homepage preview in **Settings → Landing Page** disables link clicks. Course landing previews should still be checked on the live site for enrollment flows.
</Callout>

## What students see after enrolling

After a successful enroll, students land in the [Student Dashboard](/docs/student-dashboard) (**Home** / **My Learning**). The course may disappear from **Explore** once they are a member.
