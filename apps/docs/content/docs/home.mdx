Welcome to the ClassroomIO documentation—your comprehensive guide to streamlining training for everyone. Our all-in-one platform empowers bootcamps, educators, and businesses to manage training programs easily. Run multiple classes and cohorts from a single UI with our mobile-first application, ensuring students can access all published content from any device.

## What You'll Find Here

This documentation covers everything from setup to usage, including:

- **Core Features**: Well-detailed explanations of ClassroomIO's capabilities and how to take advantage of them
- **Developer & Designer Resources**: Everything needed to successfully contribute to the project
- **Practical Guides**: From simple functionalities to complex use cases

If something isn't covered in this documentation, please feel free to [open an issue](https://github.com/classroomio/classroomio/issues) and we'll address it.
