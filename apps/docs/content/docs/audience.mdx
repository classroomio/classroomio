**Audience** is your org-wide student directory. It shows every person who belongs to your organization — whether they signed up themselves, accepted a course invite, or were added directly. From here you can search, sort, bulk-assign to courses, and manage pending invites.

:::info[Plan limits]
Your plan sets a maximum audience size. When you reach the limit, a banner appears and you cannot invite more students until you upgrade or remove existing members.
:::

## What Audience shows

Audience is separate from course-level **People**. Course People shows who is enrolled in a specific course. Audience shows everyone in your organization regardless of which courses they have joined.

| Column | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| **Name** | Student's display name |
| **Email** | Account email address |
| **Status** | Current membership state (see below) |
| **Date Joined** | When the student joined the organization |

### Status values

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Active** | Student has a verified account and is a member of your org |
| **Pending** | Invite was sent but the student has not yet accepted it |
| **Expired** | The invite link passed its expiry before the student accepted |
| **Revoked** | The invite was manually revoked by an admin |

## Assign students to courses

Select one or more students using the checkboxes, then click **Assign to Courses**. You can pick multiple courses at once. This enrolls the selected students immediately — no action needed from them.

## Manage pending invites

For students with **Pending** status, you can:

- **Resend invite** — sends a fresh invite email to that address
- **Revoke invite** — cancels the invite so the link no longer works

## Import and export

Use **Import Users** to bulk-add students from a CSV file. Use **Export** to download the current audience list.
