Why Forge

A school built around how craft is actually learned.

Practitioner-led, peer-driven, finished with work you're proud to show.

Taught by practitioners

Every instructor is a working designer or engineer shipping at a recognized studio or product team.

Weekly studio critique

Live, recorded critique with your cohort — the part of learning the craft you can't get from a video.

A real capstone

Every program ends with a portfolio-grade piece, reviewed by your instructor and three peers.

Verified certificates

Earn a Forge credential with a public verification page you can share with employers or clients.

Small cohorts

Live classes are capped at 24 learners so feedback stays specific and conversations stay real.

Alumni network

Lifetime access to the Forge community — job referrals, project critique, and city meetups.

Financial aid

Need-based scholarships and a 6-month installment plan available on every cohort program.

Forge for teams

Bring your whole team — group seats, custom cohort scheduling, and shared progress dashboards.

Open to everyone

No application gate. Pick the program, enroll, and start your work the day the cohort opens.
